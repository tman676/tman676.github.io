Thomas J. Hughes

PhD Candidate in Economics, George Mason University

I am a third-year Economics PhD candidate at George Mason University, a PhD Fellow at the Mercatus Center, and a Graduate Fellow in the F. A. Hayek Program for Advanced Study in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics. My fields are Austrian Economics and Economic Sociology and Political Economy.

My research focuses on the political economy of bureaucracy, defense and national-security policy, and expert failure. I am co-authoring The Machinery of Administration: Bureaucracy in Mainline Economics with Christopher J. Coyne. I expect to graduate in May 2027.
